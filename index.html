Specialist Watch and Clock Repair

Established 1979, We carry out full repairs and restoration to all types of watches and clocks, both antique and modern.

Services include

  • Antique dial restoration
  • Case repair and restoration
  • Antique pocket watch repairs
  • Repairs of most leading brands of watches
  • Specialising in 400 day clocks, antique and modern
  • Quartz watch repairs and batteries
